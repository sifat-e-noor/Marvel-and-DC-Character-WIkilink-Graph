The [[Marvel Universe|Marvel]] universe debuted in the pages of ''[[Fantastic Four]]'' in 1961, created by [[Stan Lee]] and [[Jack Kirby]]. At that time, ''[[Strange Tales]]'' also published stories featuring the Fantastic Four cast, mostly the [[Human Torch]] and [[Thing (comics)|Thing]], where other villains also debuted. The following is a list of antagonists that were introduced in Fantastic Four, Strange Tales and other Marvel comics. The Fantastic Four is regarded as possessing one of the strongest rogues' galleries in [[Marvel Comics]].<ref>[https://nerdist.com/article/kevin-feige-fantastic-four-x-men-marvel/ Nerdist]</ref>

==Other major villains==
