The [[Masters of Evil]] is a fictional [[supervillain]] team appearing in [[American comic book]]s published by [[Marvel Comics]]. Although the roster has changed over the years, the team remains the principal foes, and the evil counterpart, of the [[superhero]] team the [[Avengers (comics)|Avengers]].

Listed here are the known '''members''' of the Masters of Evil:

===Baron Heinrich Zemo's incarnation===
''Avengers'' #6-7, 9-10, 15-16 (July 1964 - May 1965)

* '''[[Heinrich Zemo|Baron Heinrich Zemo]]''' (leader) - He is an old enemy of Captain America from [[World War II]].
* '''The [[Black Knight (Nathan Garrett)]]''' - A descendant of [[Black Knight (Sir Percy)|Sir Percy of Scandia]] and an enemy of [[Hank Pym|Giant-Man]] who uses an arsenal of medieval weapons that employ modern technology (including a lance that fired bolts of energy) and genetically engineers and creates a winged horse called Aragorn.
* '''The [[Enchantress (Marvel Comics)#Amora|Enchantress]]''' - An Asgardian sorceress and an enemy of [[Thor (Marvel Comics)|Thor]].
* '''The [[Executioner (comics)#Skurge|Executioner]]''' - A [[half-giant]] companion of Enchantress and an enemy of Thor.
* '''The [[Melter]]''' - An enemy of [[Iron Man]] who uses melting devices.
* '''The [[Radioactive Man (comics)|Radioactive Man]]''' - A nuclear physicist with radioactive abilities and an enemy of Thor.
* '''[[Wonder Man]]''' - The owner of Williams Innovations who gains his ion-based powers from an ion ray. He sacrificed himself to save the Avengers.
